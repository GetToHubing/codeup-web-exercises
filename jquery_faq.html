Yellowstone National Park
Is the oldest U.S. national park, founded in 1872.
Yosemite National Park
Out the ten highest waterfalls in the world Yosemite national park is home to 3 out of the ten waterfalls.
Sequoia National Park
Is home to the largest living single-stem tree in the world.
Wrangell-St. Elias National Park
Is the Largest national park covering 13,000 square miles or 13.2 million acres.
Mammoth Cave National Park
Has the longest cave system in the world with more than 3,454 mapped miles.

Has the nations deepest cave which is 1,593ft deep.
Dry Tortugas National Park
Is a collection of seven tiny islands that can only be reached by boat or plane.
Hawaii Volcanoes National Park
Is home to the worlds biggest volcano.
Crater Lake National Park
Has the deepest lake at 1,932 feet deep.
